About Us

Uromi JDPCI is a Faith-based Non-Governmental Organization borne out of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi with a mandate to promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development and to provide succor for the less privileged. Our activities cover the geographical area of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi which comprises the 5 Local Government Areas in Edo Central Senatorial DFdistrict.

Project steered

Conflict Management, Training of Electorate, ElectionObservation, Budget Monitoring, Community TB care, HIV/AIDS Awareness Creation & Counseling, Micro-Credit Facility, Rural Community Development Enlightenment Campaign, Prisoners' Welfare Service, Gender And Child Rights, Widowhood Association, Human Rights, Training Programmes for Poilice Officers and Youths, Women Development & Promotion through Micro Credit, Emergency Response, Legal Aid, VC Programming, Capaigns and Sensitizations Against Human Traficking as well as Creating Alternatives to Human Traficking through Vocational Skills Acquisition and Empowerment

Our Vision

A peaceful and developed society where there is peaceful co-existence anad promorion of human rights and Empowerment of the people for meaningful living.


Our Mission

To alleviate poverty and promote integral development through the motivation and empowerment of the less privilege/oppressed and challenging unjust structures that enhances injustice, hatred and backwardness in Nigeria.
